Tibs

This is a popular traditional Ethiopian chopped beef dish

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs beef (cut in cubes)
  • 1 red onion (sliced in pieces)
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 clove of garlic (optional)
  • 2 teaspoon cooking oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Fresh rosemary
  • 1 green pepper
  • Awaze - a blend of berbere and butter/oil (optional)
